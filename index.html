The Akan Names

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were
born. These names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person
middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an
ancestor's middle name. Here are some of the Akan names:

MALE

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwa
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

FEMALE

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
