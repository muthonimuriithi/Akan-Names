The Akan Names

This application will enable you achieve your Akan name by filling in the
form with your date of birth and year and it will calculate for you your Akan
name, depending on your gender! The Akan people of Ghana frequently
name their children after the day of the week they were born. Below are how the naming
was done based on the gender:

MALE

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwa
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

FEMALE

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
